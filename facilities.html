Facilities

Library

Library is located in the main building area of the Campus and it can accommodate more than 500 students/users at a time. The collection of books (Print) is 1,33,751 including Book-Bank books, subscribes 204 Print journals for all the disciplines. We have also access to 11,814 online Journals and 8000+ eBooks. The total area is 2758.56 sq. meters including the extended floors as an additional space for reading hall.
HISTORY:
NITK Central Library established in the year 1960 is provided with modern facilities and offers automated library services to its clientele comprising of about 6000 users namely undergraduate and postgraduate students, research scholars, faculty members and supporting staff of various departments of the institute. NITK library also gives the facility of institution membership to educational institutes and industries located in and around Mangalore. This Library is located in an independent building with a carpet area of 2759 sq meters in the centre of the Campus and it can accommodate more than 500 students/users at a time

Sports

Here at NITK, extracurricular activities like sports and games are the main part of a NITK student, so NITK provides students with a wide range of sports and games to there own choice. We facilitate a state of the art sports facilities for the young minds, the college sports cricket ground, football ground, an Olympic size swimming pool, three tennis courts, two basketball courts, one hockey field, a handball court, an indoor stadium consists of three badminton courts and five T.T tables and many more. We also encourage games like carroms and chess. Many more facilities like these are gonna come when the new sports complex building will be ready. NITK also conducts so many sports events to encourage students to showcase their talents like :
Fresher's cup :
This is for the freshers of the college all twelve sections of undergraduate and first years from mtech, MCA is allowed to participate in this event they all form a different team from their respective section and compete for the grand fresher's cup which will be awarded to the section which the overall champion of the event. This event takes place for two days in your odd semester
Floodlight tournaments:
Floodlight tournaments are for everyone, every student can participate in this event.As per the name, these tournaments happen under the moonlight and facilities all kinds of sports and games. The event takes place for two days in even semester and those will be the most memorable two days of your college life
Coliseum:
Coliseum is an inter-branch sports fest held in even semester. In this, all departments come to fight for the champions title
